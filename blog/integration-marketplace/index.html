Introducing Our Integration Marketplace

Robert Kim

Partnership Manager

We're excited to announce the launch of our Integration Marketplace, featuring over 50 partner integrations that extend the functionality of our platform and connect with your favorite tools and services.

Connect Your Entire Tech Stack

Our new Integration Marketplace allows you to connect our platform with a wide range of tools across categories including:

  • CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho)
  • Marketing automation (Mailchimp, Marketo, ActiveCampaign)
  • Project management (Asana, Trello, Monday.com)
  • Communication tools (Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord)
  • File storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive)
  • Accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks)
  • E-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)
  • Customer support (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk)

Featured Integration: Slack

Our enhanced Slack integration allows you to:

  • Receive notifications about important events directly in your Slack channels
  • Create and update records without leaving Slack
  • Share dashboards and reports with your team
  • Set up custom workflows between Slack and our platform
Learn more about the Slack integration

Integration Categories

Our marketplace is organized into categories to help you find the right integrations for your needs:

Payments & Finance

Connect with payment processors, accounting software, and financial tools.

Marketing & Communication

Integrate with email marketing, social media, and communication platforms.

Security & Compliance

Connect with security tools, identity providers, and compliance solutions.

Customer Support

Integrate with help desk, live chat, and customer service platforms.

Productivity & Collaboration

Connect with project management, document sharing, and team collaboration tools.

Data & Analytics

Integrate with business intelligence, data visualization, and analytics platforms.

Developer Tools

For developers looking to build custom integrations, we've enhanced our developer tools:

  • Improved API documentation with interactive examples
  • Webhook management interface for easier configuration
  • SDKs for popular programming languages (JavaScript, Python, Ruby, PHP)
  • Integration templates to accelerate development
  • Testing tools to validate your integrations

Partner Program

Are you interested in becoming an integration partner? Our partner program offers:

  • Technical support for integration development
  • Co-marketing opportunities
  • Revenue sharing for premium integrations
  • Early access to new API features
Learn more about our partner program

Getting Started

To start using the Integration Marketplace:

  1. Navigate to the new "Integrations" section in your dashboard
  2. Browse or search for the tools you want to connect
  3. Click "Install" and follow the authorization process
  4. Configure the integration settings to match your workflow
  5. Start using the connected tools together

We're continuously adding new integrations to the marketplace. If there's a specific integration you'd like to see, please let us know through our feature request form.

We're excited to see how these integrations help streamline your workflows and look forward to your feedback as you explore the marketplace.
